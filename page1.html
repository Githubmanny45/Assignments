  1. CSS

  2. HTML
    1. HTML stands for Hyper Text Markup Language
    2. HTML describes the content of the page, your lines of code providing sctructure to the information you'd like displayed.
    3. You may choose from a variety of tags, such as heading, paragraph, ordered and/or unordered list, header and footer tags, and many more!
  3. Images/Links
    1. Images and/or links can be embedded into your webpage with the correct formatting.
